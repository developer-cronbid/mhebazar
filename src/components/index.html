Visit IWE Mumbai - Intralogistics & Warehousing Expo

South Asia's Biggest Warehouse Infra, Storage, Material Handling, Automation, Logistics & Supply Chain Expo

Mumbai, India: IWE Mumbai - Intralogistics & Warehousing Expo 2026 will be held from 14–16 May 2026 at Bombay Exhibition Centre, Mumbai. The event will bring together companies, experts, and professionals from the logistics, warehousing, and supply chain industries. Organized by Future Market Events, the expo is one of India's leading platforms for showcasing the latest technologies and solutions in warehouse management, intralogistics, automation, and supply chain operations.

Now in its 5th edition, the exhibition will feature over 275 exhibitors and showcase more than 1,500 products and solutions related to warehousing, logistics automation, material handling equipment, robotics, storage systems, industrial infrastructure, and supply chain technologies. The event is expected to attract thousands of visitors including logistics companies, manufacturers, retailers, e-commerce brands, pharmaceutical companies, distributors, warehouse developers, investors, and supply chain professionals from across India and overseas markets.

Featured Exhibitors

Several leading companies have already confirmed their participation as exhibitors. These include SOTI, Gandhi Automations, Action Construction Equipment (ACE), NIDO Machineries, Newgentech, Muvro Technologies, Jemkon, INDSTALL Sustainable Structures, Honeywell, Swift Technoplast, Sameer Electro Systems, Nexilon Energy Systems, Arcedges Building India, Aguatech Engineers, Reliable Storage Systems, APL Apollo Tubes, Saifi Con-Fab System, Seimetsu Factory Automation, Kusum Group, Consolidated Swift Industries, Aara Energy Innovations, IE Pallets, Mega Link Chains, Assa Abloy, Superior Weldmesh, Stuti Technologies, Maini Materials Movement, Speciality Urethanes, SFS Equipments and many more industry leaders. These exhibitors will showcase advanced solutions designed to improve warehouse productivity, optimize storage capacity, reduce operational costs, strengthen supply chain resilience, and accelerate automation across industries.

Co-Located Industry Shows

The expo will be held alongside two important co-located exhibitions – Material Handling Expo and Pharma Supply Chain Expo. Bringing these shows together will create a powerful industry platform where visitors can explore complete solutions across logistics, warehousing, automation, pharma distribution, and material handling under one roof.

Summits, Leadership Forums & Networking Events

A major highlight of Intralogistics & Warehousing Expo 2026 will be its specially curated conferences, conclaves, leadership dialogues, and business networking events. These platforms are designed to encourage collaboration, strategic partnerships, and knowledge exchange among senior professionals, investors, policymakers, technology providers, and business leaders.

Warehousing & Supply Chain Summit

A high-level conference focused on the latest trends, challenges, and innovations in warehousing, logistics, fulfillment, and supply chain efficiency. Industry leaders will discuss digital transformation, automation, cost optimization, last-mile delivery, sustainability, and resilience strategies.

Date: May 15, 2026 · Mumbai

Pharma Supply Chain Summit

Held under the theme "Powering Pharma Supply Chain: From Strategy to Execution," this summit will focus on regulatory compliance, cold chain logistics, inventory visibility, risk mitigation, and operational excellence in pharmaceutical supply chains.

Date: May 14, 2026 · Mumbai

Warehouse Developers & Leasing Conclave

A focused forum exploring opportunities in industrial real estate, warehousing parks, build-to-suit facilities, leasing demand, infrastructure investments, and future growth of India's warehouse ecosystem.

Date: May 15, 2026 · Mumbai

CEO Roundtable & Leadership Dialogues

An exclusive closed-door platform featuring senior business leaders and policymakers discussing future industry trends, infrastructure priorities, policy outlook, investment opportunities, and leadership strategies shaping India's logistics sector.

Date: May 15, 2026 · Mumbai

Open House Sessions & Workshops

Interactive sessions designed for learning, collaboration, live demonstrations, technology showcases, and solving real-world warehousing and supply chain challenges. These sessions will provide practical takeaways for attendees across industries.

Date: May 14–16, 2026 · Mumbai
